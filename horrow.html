What Makes A Horror Movie?

Horror movies are designed to create fear, dread, suspense, and discomfort through atmosphere, danger, the unknown, and emotional tension.

Popular Horror Subgenres

Common horror styles include supernatural horror, slasher horror, psychological horror, monster horror, survival horror, and possession horror.

Why People Love Horror Films

Horror films are exciting because they create adrenaline, tension, mystery, and unforgettable moments that stay with viewers long after the credits.
