Comedy movies are designed to entertain through humor, awkward situations, strong personalities, misunderstandings, timing, and memorable dialogue.

Comedy films are easy to rewatch, fun to share with others, and great for relaxing because they deliver laughter, energy, and unforgettable scenes.
