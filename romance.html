What Makes A Sci-Fi Movie?

Sci-fi movies imagine futures, technology, alien life, alternate realities, scientific possibilities, and big questions about humanity and survival.

Popular Sci-Fi Subgenres

Common sci-fi styles include space sci-fi, AI sci-fi, dystopian sci-fi, alien sci-fi, survival sci-fi, and mind-bending science fiction.

Why People Love Sci-Fi Films

Sci-fi films are loved because they mix spectacle, imagination, mystery, emotion, and huge ideas that push beyond everyday reality.
