2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember information about your visit, device, preferences, and activity over time.

Cookies can make websites work more efficiently and can also be used for analytics, personalization, and advertising purposes.

3. How We May Use Cookies

MovieSpot may use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Keep the website functioning properly
  • Remember user preferences
  • Understand how visitors use the site
  • Measure website traffic and performance
  • Improve content and user experience
  • Support advertising and third-party services

4. Types of Cookies We May Use

a) Essential cookies

These cookies are necessary for the basic operation of the website. Without them, some parts of MovieSpot may not function correctly.

b) Performance and analytics cookies

These cookies help us understand how users interact with the website, such as which pages are visited most often and how visitors move through the site.

c) Functionality cookies

These cookies may remember choices you make, such as preferences or settings, to improve your browsing experience.

d) Advertising cookies

These cookies may be used by advertising partners to show ads that are more relevant to your interests or to measure advertising effectiveness.

5. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies may be set by third-party services that appear on MovieSpot, such as analytics providers, advertisers, embedded media, or external platforms.

Examples may include:

These third parties may collect information in line with their own privacy and cookies policies.

6. Managing Cookies

You can usually control, block, or delete cookies through your browser settings. Each browser provides different ways to manage cookie preferences.

You may be able to:

  • Delete stored cookies

  • Allow only certain cookies
  • Receive alerts before cookies are stored

Please note that disabling some cookies may affect site performance or prevent certain parts of MovieSpot from functioning properly.
